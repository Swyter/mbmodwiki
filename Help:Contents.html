[edit] Basic Help and Guidelines

This web page is structured as a fully editable Wiki, that means that it's shown as structured documentation pages that you can enhance.

We always recommend to start by the checking the Basics Section, later deploy your tools with the help of the Official docs. Later, feel free to experiment with the tools and Open Source scripts and Open Source Packs of models and animations, that comes in handy when creating your fist mod.

Once you're a experienced craftsman guru, collaborate bringing more articles to the great public.

Have fun!
--The Modding Wiki Staff

The information contained here is provided for free, but without any guaranty, we do our best for sharing with you our knowledge. Help us in the task!
